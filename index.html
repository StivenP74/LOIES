‚ö° What is a LOIE?

A LOIE (Loss Of Electricity Incident) occurs when, due to an error on our part, a customer ends up being disconnected from their electricity service. These incidents negatively impact customer satisfaction and service quality.

LOIE incidents have significant negative impacts:

  • Affects the performance metrics of our supervisors
  • Decreases our Quality Assurance ratings
  • Impacts the overall campaign performance
  • Most importantly, it causes unnecessary hardship to customers

üí° How to Avoid LOIE?

Carefully review disconnection dates. Pay special attention to the following details:

  • If the extension date for past due balance is the same as the expiration date of the complete invoice, the customer may still be at risk of disconnection
  • Don't give the customer false expectations about their service status
  • Double-check all information before confirming payment arrangements
  • When in doubt, consult with a supervisor or use the available tools
